CinnamonRolls Recipe

Indgredients

  • 360g pkt store-bought bake at home croissants, thawed
  • 1/3 cup cinnamon sugar
  • 100g Caramilk chocolate, melted, cooled
  • 100ml vanilla custard
  • 1/2 cup Crunchy Nut cornflakes, lightly crushed

Steps

  1. Roll out croissants and place shiny-side up on a flat surface, gently stretching the dough. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Roll up and cut in half. Place cut-side down and insert your index finger into the centre to flatten and stretch the scroll.

  2. Arrange scrolls, cut-side down, into the basket of an air fryer, allowing for a 1cm gap between each scroll. You may need to do this in 2 batches.

  3. Air fry at 170C for 7 minutes or until golden. Leave to sit in the hot air fryer for a further 5 minutes (see note). Transfer to a serving plate.

  4. Drizzle with Caramilk and custard, and sprinkle over Crunchy Nut corn flakes. Watch our step-by-step video below to see how to make these Air fryer Caramilk cinnamon rolls.
