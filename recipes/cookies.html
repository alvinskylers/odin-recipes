Cookies Recipe

Indgredients

  • 150g (1 cup) plain flour
  • 150g (1 cup) self-raising flour
  • 220g (1 cup) caster sugar
  • 190g (1 cup) dark Choc Bits
  • 125g butter, melted
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C/160°C fan forced. Line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.

  2. Sift the plain flour and self-raising flour into a medium bowl. Add the sugar and Choc Bits and stir well to combine.

  3. Place butter and egg in a small bowl and mix thoroughly. Pour into the flour mixture and stir until combined.

  4. Place the dough between 2 sheets of baking paper and roll out to 5mm thickness. Place in the fridge for 20 minutes. Using a 6cm round cutter, cut rounds, re rolling to use up all the dough and place on baking trays. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until the biscuits spread slightly and are light golden.

  5. Remove from oven and set aside to cool completely on the trays. Repeat with remaining biscuit mixture.
