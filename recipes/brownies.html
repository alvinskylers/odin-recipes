Brownies Recipe


Ingredients Recipe

  • 100g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 100g butter, chopped
  • 1 cup caster sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp Baileys Irish Cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 2/3 cup of walnuts, chopped

    • Bailys Ganache

  • 150g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Baileys Irish Cream
  • 2/3 cup thickend cream

Steps

  1. Place chocolate and butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on HIGH (100%) for 1 minute or until melted. Stir in sugar, eggs, , vanilla, our and 1/2 cup walnuts. Pour into a 4cm-deep, 23cm square microwave-safe silicone cake pan.

  2. Microwave on HIGH (100%) for 6 minutes or until just cooked in the centre. Stand for 5 minutes in microwave. Cool completely in pan. Turn, top-side up, onto a board lined with baking paper.

  3. Microwave on HIGH (100%) for 6 minutes or until just cooked in the centre. Stand for 5 minutes in microwave. Cool completely in pan. Turn, top-side up, onto a board lined with baking paper.

  4. Spread brownie with ganache. Sprinkle with remaining walnuts. Cut brownie into 16 pieces. Serve
